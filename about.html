Learning

I learned (and am still learning!) photography primarily through free online resources and books. I am entirely self-taught, and have never paid for a class. Some of the resources I have used are linked below.

Udemy Course: Your Road to Better Photography

Really good, free introductory course. This is for people who own a DSLR camera, have a small idea of how it works, but struggle when using it on full manual. Also introduces you to basics of composition and some great tips on lighting.

Reddit Photoclass: Intro to Photography

Created by Alex Buisse, reddit photoclass is a free course with '30 lessons covering most of the basic subjects, assuming no prior knowledge, and with a focus on the practical over theoretical explanations'.

Adobe TV: Getting Started with Adobe Lightroom

A great introduction to the basics of Lightroom (yes, I still use version 5!)
